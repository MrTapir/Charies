Enemies

There are currently 8 enemies in Charies

Here is the list of them

Level 1 enemies

Dwarf

  • A weak melee enemy with a short-range weapon.
  • Deals minimal damage and has low health.

Evil Golem

  • A ranged enemy that uses fireballs as its attack.
  • It has the lowest health among the enemies in Level 1 and appears to hover due to its unique design.

Slayer

  • A slow, towering melee enemy.
  • Deals the highest single-attack damage among regular enemies in this level.

Mechanical Golem

  • The boss of Level 1 with high health and multiple attack types.
  • Performs melee attacks when approached or long-range attacks like lasers and falling rocks.

Level 2 enemies

Fort Golem

  • A melee enemy with a unique attack sequence of three hits.
  • Damage is distributed across all three attacks rather than a single strike.

Dark Elf

  • A support enemy that heals other nearby enemies instead of directly attacking the player.
  • Finds and assists allies using a specialized healing ability.

Medusa

  • A hybrid enemy with melee and ranged attacks.
  • Fires explosive projectiles from her staff and is harder to hit due to her crouched posture.

Anubis

  • The final boss with high attack damage but moderate health.
  • Uses close-range sand attacks to reduce visibility and summons obelisks that fire projectiles.
