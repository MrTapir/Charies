Summary

Charies is a 3D first-person roguelike fantasy game where players progress through procedurally generated levels filled with enemies, traps, and rewards. Each level culminates in a boss fight, and defeating the boss grants access to the next level. Players use magic-based combat, equipping up to two spells at a time, such as Fireball or Flamethrower, to defeat a variety of enemies with unique behaviors. Rewards like coins, health, and upgrades are earned by clearing rooms, and the ultimate goal is to defeat the final boss and escape the dungeon. The game emphasizes strategic planning and skillful use of abilities in a dynamic environment.
