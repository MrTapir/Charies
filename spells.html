Spells

There are currently 5 spells in Charies

Here is the list of them

Flamethrower

  • A close-range spell that deals continuous damage to enemies with fire particles.
  • Inflicts a burning effect, causing additional damage over time.

Crystal Shatter

  • Fires a line of purple crystals, with the final crystal dealing the main damage upon impact or at maximum range
  • The crystals remain in place after spawning and do not follow the player.

Ground Crack

  • Creates a shockwave effect around the player, stunning enemies and dealing minor damage.
  • Useful for crowd control and temporarily immobilizing foes.

Fireball

  • A ranged attack spell.
  • Direct hits deal significant damage, while nearby enemies take reduced damage from the explosion.

Laser Beam

  • A charged attack where holding and releasing the right mouse button fires a powerful blue laser.
  • Damage increases the longer the spell is charged.
